Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a transfer?

You can easily reserve your ride through our online form. Alternatively, we are also available by phone or via WhatsApp.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver will automatically adjust to the new arrival time ‚Äî at no extra cost.

Are child seats available?

Yes, we provide free child seats on request. Simply mention it when making your booking.

Which payment methods are accepted?

You can conveniently pay by credit card, Twint, invoice (for business clients), or in cash with the chauffeur.

How last-minute can I book?

Bookings are usually possible up to 2 hours before departure. For urgent rides, we recommend contacting us by phone.

Do you only operate in Switzerland?

No, we also provide international transfers upon request (e.g., Germany, France, Italy).
