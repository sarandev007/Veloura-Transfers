Frequently Asked Questions ‚Äì Airport & Hotel Transfers

How does the pickup at Zurich Airport (ZRH) work?

Your chauffeur will wait for you at the official Meeting Point inside the arrival hall. For private jet arrivals, pickup takes place at the GAC / General Aviation Center. Included: Meet & Greet, flight tracking, and luggage assistance.

Do you track my flight?

Yes. We monitor your flight in real time. If your flight arrives earlier or later than planned, your chauffeur adjusts automatically with no additional charges.

Can I book an airport or hotel transfer at short notice?

Yes, short-notice bookings are usually possible. For immediate transfers we recommend contacting us via phone or WhatsApp.

Which vehicles are available for Airport & Hotel Transfers?

Mercedes S-Class, E-Class, V-Class, SUVs such as GLS / GLE, and Sprinter vans for groups. All vehicles are modern, clean, and fully equipped.

Do you provide luggage assistance?

Yes. Our chauffeurs assist with loading and unloading your luggage ‚Äî including large suitcases, business bags, and sports equipment.

How does the pickup at the hotel work?

The chauffeur waits in front of the hotel or ‚Äî where permitted ‚Äî directly at the entrance. In premium hotels, we also accompany you to the lobby or reception upon request.

How many passengers can travel?

From 1 up to 16 guests. Limousines for individuals or couples, vans for families or business teams, and Sprinters for group or event transfers.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, Twint, cash, Apple Pay, Google Pay and invoice payment for corporate clients.
